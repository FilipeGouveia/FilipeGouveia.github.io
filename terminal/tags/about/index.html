About

2019-01-25 :: Filipe Gouveia
#about 
I have been a Research Associate, during 2023-2024, at the Department of Computer Science of the University of Oxford in the Logic and Learning (LOL) group, in the field of Inductive Logic Programming. I was also a researcher at INESC-ID Lisboa in the Automated Reasoning and Software Reliability (ARSR) group, during 2016-2023. I obtained an MSc in Information Systems and Computer Engineering from IST - Universidade de Lisboa in 2012, focussing on intelligent systems, and information technology.
